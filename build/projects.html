Amori is a short, story-centered game heavily inspired by the game Omori made by Omocat. It was the final project for my grade 10 computer science culminating project. Me and my parter had a lot of fun using processing to its fullest capabilities to make a game that we both enjoyed at the end.

LearnAi.js

LearnAI.js is a javascript library I helped make for my grade 11 computer science final project. The AI was developed by us to help other students learn about AI, and help teachers teach students about AI in a more fun and creative way. The base AI was trained to detect hand-drawn numbers.

Inbot

Inbot is a discord bot that I helped make for a Lyonhacks III, a hackathon event. The purpose of Inbot is to help students stay on track and get regular notifications of their emails all in discord. Personally, I can find it hard to check my emails regularly, so having a bot send you your emails to a place you frequently visit helps with productivity.
